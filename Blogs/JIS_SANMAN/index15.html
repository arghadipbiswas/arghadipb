JIS Sanman 2023: A Celebration of Excellence at Science City Auditorium


A Day to Remember

The JIS Sanman 2023 event at Science City Auditorium in Kolkata was a day of immense pride and celebration for students across the JIS Group of colleges. This prestigious annual event is organized to honor and recognize outstanding achievements in academics, extracurricular activities, innovative projects, and sports.

My team and I had the incredible honor of being invited to receive the Best Student Project Award. The event was not only a celebration of our hard work and dedication but also an opportunity to come together with students from various JIS colleges and revel in a day filled with excitement and recognition.

Honoring Innovation: Receiving the Best Student Project Award

The highlight of the day for us was receiving the Best Student Project Award. Our team had worked tirelessly on our project, pouring countless hours into research, development, and fine-tuning. To have our efforts recognized on such a grand stage was truly exhilarating. As we walked up to the stage to receive our prize, the sense of accomplishment was overwhelming.

The recognition from the JIS Group not only validated our hard work but also inspired us to continue pushing the boundaries of innovation. This award is a testament to the dedication and teamwork that went into our project, and it will undoubtedly motivate us to achieve even greater things in the future.

A Star-Studded Event: Sara Ali Khan’s Presence

Adding a touch of glamour to the academic achievements, the event was graced by the presence of Bollywood actress Sara Ali Khan. Her appearance was one of the most anticipated moments of the day, and she did not disappoint. Sara’s presence brought an extra layer of excitement to the event, as she interacted with the students and shared her thoughts on the importance of balancing academics with extracurricular activities

Her words were both encouraging and inspiring, reminding us that success is not just about hard work but also about passion, creativity, and perseverance. Meeting her in person was an unforgettable experience and added a unique flair to the celebration.

A Gathering of Talents: Students from Across JIS Group

The JIS Sanman 2023 event was a melting pot of talent, with students from all JIS Group colleges coming together to celebrate their achievements. It was a fantastic opportunity to meet peers from different campuses, exchange ideas, and learn about the innovative projects and activities being undertaken across the group.

The atmosphere was electric, filled with the energy and enthusiasm of students eager to showcase their talents. The event also highlighted the diverse range of skills and achievements within the JIS community, from academic excellence to groundbreaking projects and sporting prowess. Being part of such a vibrant and dynamic gathering was truly inspiring.

The JIS Sanman 2023 event at Science City Auditorium was more than just an award ceremony; it was a celebration of the hard work, creativity, and dedication of students across the JIS Group. From receiving the Best Student Project Award to enjoying the star-studded presence of Sara Ali Khan, the day was filled with moments of pride and joy. It was also a reminder of the importance of balancing academic pursuits with extracurricular activities and the impact of innovative thinking. As we look back on this memorable event, we are filled with gratitude for the recognition and motivation to continue striving for excellence in all our future endeavors.
