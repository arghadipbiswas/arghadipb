Celebrating Excellence: Arghadip Biswas Wins the Kriti Award 2022


A Moment of Pride

In the ever-evolving landscape of technology and innovation, certain achievements shine brightly, marking the milestones of one’s journey. I am incredibly proud to share that I have been honored with the Kriti Award 2022 for Outstanding Achievement of the Year.

This prestigious award is a recognition of my hard work, perseverance, and the success achieved as a member of Team Amateurs, the winning team at the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2022 Software Edition. As a student from the CSE Department of Guru Nanak Institute of Technology, this accolade holds significant importance in my academic and professional journey.

A Heartwarming Invitation: Recognition from Our Principal

One of the most unforgettable moments of this journey was the special recognition given by our principal. Following our victory at the Smart India Hackathon, our principal extended a heartfelt invitation to us and our parents to formally receive the award at a special ceremony. This event was more than just a formality; it was a celebration of our hard work, dedication, and the unwavering support from our families.

As we gathered for the award presentation, the room was filled with a palpable sense of pride and accomplishment. After the ceremony, we were treated to a delightful lunch, where we shared stories, laughter, and the joy of our achievement. This gesture by our principal made the moment truly historic, etching it into my memory as one of the most significant days of my life.

A Moment of Pride: Making My Parents Proud

Standing beside my parents while receiving the Kriti Award from our principal was an emotionally overwhelming experience. This was not just my achievement; it was a moment that symbolized the sacrifices, encouragement, and unwavering support of my parents throughout my journey. As I looked into their eyes, filled with pride and joy, I felt an indescribable sense of fulfillment.

In that moment, I realized that all the late nights, the struggles, and the hard work had finally borne fruit—not just for me, but for my parents as well. Seeing them so proud made me feel like I had finally given something back to them, a tangible proof that their belief in me was justified. This recognition was not just an award; it was a validation of the efforts that we, as a family, had collectively invested in my future.

The Path to Success: Journey of Team Amateurs

When the results were announced and Team Amateurs was declared the winner of the SIH Senior The journey to winning the Smart India Hackathon was nothing short of a rollercoaster. Team Amateurs, which I was privileged to be a part of, embarked on a mission to tackle a pressing issue: Tracking, reducing, and reusing plastic waste. With PSID: SG864 as our guiding light, we spent 36 hours coding, brainstorming, and refining our solution. The challenge was immense, but so was the drive within each team member. Our efforts culminated in a victory that not only brought us the SIH title but also laid the foundation for the Kriti Award.

Recognition Beyond Victory: The Kriti Award

The Kriti Award 2022 for Outstanding Achievement of the Year is more than just a trophy; it symbolizes the recognition of excellence in the field of technology and innovation. Receiving this award is a testament to the dedication and hard work that went into the project, and it reflects the collective effort of the team, my mentors, and the support from Guru Nanak Institute of Technology. This award is a proud moment not just for me but for everyone who contributed to the journey.
